Professional development in cybersecurity is a constant treadmill; the industry is always changing, attackers find new exploits every day, and cybersecurity programs continue to get more complex (and convoluted!) as time goes on.

Podcasts are a fantastic way to keep up with the moving cybersecurity landscape, as well as with a variety of topics and interests. The following are some favorites of the Montana Cyber Team, organized by domain. These are not ordered in any particular fashion, and no official endorsement of these organizations is implied or specified.

## Domestic and Foreign Policy, Law and Governance

- [The President's Inbox](https://www.cfr.org/podcasts/president's-inbox)
- [The Lawfare Daily](https://www.lawfaremedia.org/podcasts-multimedia/podcast/the-lawfare-podcast)
- [Caveat, CyberWire's law and policy podcast](https://www.thecyberwire.com/podcasts/caveat)

## News

- [CyberWire Daily Intel brief](https://thecyberwire.com/podcasts/daily-podcast)
